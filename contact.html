How to Update Contact Information

To add your real contact details:

  1. Go to your GitHub repository at github.com/Marsdirt/warnerclassic-website
  2. Click on contact.html
  3. Click the pencil icon to edit
  4. Replace the placeholder email, phone, and location with your real information
  5. Scroll down and click "Commit changes"
  6. Your website will update automatically in about 1 minute!
